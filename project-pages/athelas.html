Athelas

Graphic & Layout Design

One of my favorite freelance projects (let me know if you'd like to see more), which I completed for Athelas, an at-home blood testing device made for cancer patients. For this project, I worked closely with the founders to create an easy understand instruction & subscription model manuals for their users.



Instruction Manual

The graphics were completed in Illustrator, and the layout in InDesign.

Subscription Manual

The subscription manual is a modified instruction manual — the additional graphics were created in Illustrator and adapted to the existing InDesign layout.
