Technicolor Beat Lyrics Video

Graphic Design & Motion Graphics

For this Iovine and Young project highlight, I illustrated and animated a short lyric video for Oh Wonder’s “Technicolor Beat.” I used Adobe Illustrator to layout and create all of the slides and After Effects to animate them.



As you may know by now, I'm Mimi, a designer & illustrator from sunny Bay Area currently based in even sunnier Los Angeles. I'm learning about product design and data visualization through a project based curriculum at the Iovine and Young Academy at USC.

Find some of my code on Github & some of my digital art on Instagram, or ping me by e-mail if you'd like to chat!
