Digital Art in Illustrator

This past 2017 summer I declined internship opportunities in order to focus on my physical and mental health. Knowing that my peers were moving forward with professional opportunities I was giving up for hours of doctor’s visits and group therapy made me uneasy with my choice. Retrospectively, I’ve learned that health is a valid and critical choice, one that I maintain today and has helped me dive into creating my most productive and thoughtful work. With a good deal of free time on my hands, I launched Break to Make and started a daily art Instagram page (linked here and below) where I posted experimentation in digital and traditional art. These vector portraits of my friends were some of my favorite pieces — and I got really good at using the pen tool. :^)
