Ideas to Impact

Graphic Design

From the 2017 spring through the summer I developed graphics and building a curriculum for USC’s first student-led class. From Ideas to Impact is a 2-unit project-based course that helps students through the process of having a measured, mindful, and effective impact at USC. The curriculum is heavily based on design-thinking methodologies from IDEO, d.school, and +Acumen. I’ve developped a brand identity for the class, with an accompanying brand book and digital marketing materials.



The Brand

Logo Iteration

The graphics team and I used the cicada and its three stages of growth to convey the process of taking an idea to fruition. The final logo (right column, bottom) appears in its long, normal, and large logotype form.

Brand Book

In addition to the logo, I put together a brand book to document the classe's mission and how it should be conveyed with the brand.

Marketing Flyers

This flyer and its variants was created to reach out to students on campus to join the class.
