KPCB Redesign

For the 2018 KPCB Design Fellows Application, I've chosen to redesign a small feature in Slack. I chose Slack since I've used it extensively in the past year for projects, ranging from a 4 person workspace to a 100+ team workspace. It's been interesting to see Slack's usability evolve as the size of the team scales, and I couldn't resist the challenge of tackling problems I (and my teammates) experience while we use Slack.



The Problem

The primary problem my teams have run into while using Slack are tied to its nature as a messaging platform — messages can sometimes get lost in a sea of channels, which makes it difficult to actively participate in conversations, or more specifically for Joanna's use case, challenging to establish a smooth design pipeline between designers and project leads requesting assets.
