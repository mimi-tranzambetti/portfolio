TechLA Fellows

Graphic Design, Webdev, layout

As part of a Spark SC initiative, I worked on a fellowship program to connect talented CS, Design, and Business students to promising Los Angeles Startups. I created digital marketing assets for social media as well as flyers, swag, sponsorship decks, a website, and a 48-page guide for incoming interns to local favorites in LA.



The Brand

TechLA Fellows connects talented undergraduates studying engineering, design, and business with Los Angeles’ most innovative startups for a 10-week summer internship experience. We expose students to the opportunities available in Los Angeles, connect companies with otherwise undiscovered students, and show that Los Angeles can stand toe-to-toe with any major tech hub in the country. As such, our colors reflect LA's bright hues & the logo encapsulates the growing startup ecosystem.
