Batlló

Fashion Design

For this materials studies project, I wanted to learn more about textiles and was inspired by Gaudi’s architecture. My final product was a handsewn shirt with a pop of color reminiscent of Gaudi’s Casa Batlló. The project was a month long, from creating the shirt and brand to the final lasercut packaging.



As you may know by now, I'm a Mimi, a designer & illustrator from sunny Bay Area currently based in even sunnier Los Angeles. I'm learning about product design and data visualization through a project based curriculum at the Iovine and Young Academy at USC.

Find some of my code on Github & some of my digital art on Instagram, or ping me by e-mail if you'd like to chat!
